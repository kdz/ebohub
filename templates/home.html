About EboHub

Text #help to 512-942-0915 to get started ... assuming the Twilio account still has some money left in it!

EboHub is an SMS-based service for an Ebola-striken area with a population using primarily feature-phones, not smart-phones. This web site itself is mostly meant for administrators rather than general people using smart phones or desktop browsers, although it could be quite easily re-purposed for such usage.
